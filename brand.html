ABOUT GLAM

GLAM is a cosmetics brand launched in September 2017. The brand is popular for its broad inclusivity across skin tones and gender, especially its Pro Filt'R foundation.
GLAM was named to Time magazine's list of the 25 best inventions of 2017,alongside NASA's Martian spacecraft InSight,Apple's iPhone X,Nike's Pro Hijab,and the Tesla Model 3.
GLAM also won the WWD Beauty Inc. award in 2017 for launch of the year in the prestige sector.
